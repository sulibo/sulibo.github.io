About

I am a senior computer scientist at Institute of Experimental Psychophysiology - Düsseldorf, working on developing machine learning based algorithms for affective computing and relevant research software. Before this, I was a PhD candidate in the NUMA (Numerical Analysis and Applied Mathematics) Research unit of Department of Computer Science, KU Leuven and the Dynamics and Control group of Department of Mechanical Engineering, Eindhoven University of Technology. Meanwhile, I was also an early stage researcher (ESR 3) in an EU Marie Sklodowska-Curie Innovative Training Networks (ITN-EJD) project UCoCoS. During my PhD, I was working on partial synchronization in networks of delay-coupled systems. The objective was to analyze the robustness and parametric dependence of clusters when delay-coupled networks are paritially synchronized. I obtained my master degree from Eindhoven University of Technology, the Netherlands. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Zhejiang Universtiy, China.
