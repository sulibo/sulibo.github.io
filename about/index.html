About

I am a PhD candidate in the Scientific Computing Research Group of Department of Computer Science, KU Leuven and the Dynamics and Control group of Department of Mechanical Engineering, Eindhoven University of Technology. Meanwhile, I am also an early stage researcher (ESR 3) in an EU Marie Sklodowska-Curie Innovative Training Networks (ITN-EJD) project UCoCoS. I am working on partial synchronization in delay-coupled networks. The objective is to analyze the robustness and parametric dependence of clusters when delay-coupled networks are paritially synchronized. I obtained my master degree from Eindhoven University of Technology, the Netherlands. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Zhejiang Universtiy, China.
